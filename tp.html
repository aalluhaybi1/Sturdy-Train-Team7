This page provides various transportation options including Uber, car rental services, and a shuttle service. You can also find contact information at the bottom of the page.

UNA provides free airport transportation service from the Huntsville International Airport (HSV) ONLY and only on specific dates prior to each semester/term. To sign up for this service, send the details of your flight (in English) to oiaservices@una.edu. You will be driven to Florence, Alabama, and brought to a local hotel. You are responsible to pay for one to three nights in the hotel before you can move into the university residences or apartments.
